TRONIC ESPORT CHAMPIONSHIP
Tronic Esport Championship is the first-ever virtual gaming competition organized by the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering of University of Moratuwa. It adds one more glorious event to the ENTC calendar marking the start of the new year on a high note. Competition is open for all the undergraduates of ENTC department and alumni members inviting everyone for an interactive and amusing episode of gaming.
